English & Arabic Typewriting Syllabus

Increase typing speed and accuracy in both English and Arabic for professional, legal, and official documentation.

Duration 4 Weeks
Format Offline
Certification Certificate of Completion

Course Curriculum

Week 1 Keyboard Familiarization (English)

Learn the basics of touch typing in English without looking at the keyboard.

  • Home Row Positioning
  • Finger Placement and Posture
  • Basic Keystroke Exercises
  • Building Muscle Memory
Week 2 Keyboard Familiarization (Arabic)

Transition your typing skills to the Arabic keyboard layout.

  • Arabic Keyboard Layout Overview
  • Typing Arabic Letters and Ligatures
  • Vowels (Tashkeel) and Special Characters
  • Basic Arabic Typing Exercises
Week 3 Speed and Accuracy Building

Focus on reducing errors while significantly increasing words per minute (WPM).

  • Timed Typing Drills (English & Arabic)
  • Error Reduction Strategies
  • Typing Complex Formatting and Numbers
  • Paragraph and Essay Typing
Week 4 Professional Formatting & Certification

Apply your typing skills to real-world professional documents and complete the final speed test.

  • Formatting Business Letters and Memos
  • Typing Legal and Official Documents
  • Final WPM Speed and Accuracy Test
  • Course Review and Certification
